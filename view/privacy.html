Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/01/2025

At Two-D Infra, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and property preferences when you submit an inquiry.
  • Transactional Information: Property listings you interact with, services you request, and communications with us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and website usage analytics to improve user experience.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate property transactions and services.
  • Improve our website and services based on user behavior.
  • Send relevant updates, property alerts, and promotional offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • Comply with legal obligations.
3. Information Sharing
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.
  • We may share your data with trusted third parties (such as legal advisors or payment processors) only when necessary for service fulfillment.
  • In case of legal obligations, we may disclose information as required by law.
4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections or deletion of inaccurate information.
  • Opt out of promotional emails at any time.
7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Continued use of our services after modifications implies acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at contact@twodinfra.in.
